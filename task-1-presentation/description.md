For this task, I created a digital literacy awareness infographic using Canva. The infographic explains what digital literacy is and why it is important for students.

I included topics such as safe internet practices, useful digital tools, and maintaining a professional online presence. The design was simple and easy to understand so that students can quickly learn the key points.

While making the infographic, I found it interesting to use design elements like icons and colors. However, arranging the content properly in one page was a bit challenging. Overall, this task helped me understand how to present information visually.

Poor digital communication can create misunderstandings and problems. For example, sending an email without a proper subject line or respectful tone can create a bad impression.
In many cases, informal language or missing information leads to confusion. This can affect relationships between students, teachers, and professionals.
Such problems can be avoided by writing clear and polite emails with proper structure. Using subject lines, greetings, and respectful language is very important. Good communication helps in building a positive image and avoiding misunderstandings.

# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips

- Never share OTP, passwords, or bank details with anyone  
- Always check the website URL before entering information  
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links  
- Enable two-factor authentication for accounts  
- Use strong and unique passwords  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for online payments  
- Always verify UPI payment requests before approving  
- Keep your apps and devices updated  
- Report cybercrime on cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call helpline 1930 in case of fraud
